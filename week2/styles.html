Amrita vishawvidyapeetam

Mata Amritanandamayi Devi (born sudhaamni idamannnel on september 27, 1953),
 is an indian hindu spiritual leader and guru who is revered as a saint by her followers.
 She is often referred to as amma (mother) by her devotees.
